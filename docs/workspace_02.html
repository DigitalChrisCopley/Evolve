Test Workspace

Accessibility

Within this workspace we are testing for accessibility, in particular we are looking at the following:

  1. Whether or not the screen reader can accurately read the content
  2. Whether we can tab through the content
  3. How well the reader performs with hidden accessibilty tags

Other Factors

We will also be considering other factors

  • How well the reader performs with ordered and unordered lists
  • Whether or not we need to use aria tags or titles for links
