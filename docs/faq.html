Microsoft Teams

Microsoft Teams has many great functions including, video conference, voice conference, chat facilities, file share, collaboration, white boarding etc.

To support the accelerated rollout of teams in phase one, we will only be enabling video, voice and chat functionality.

Additional functionality will be added in due course.

In agreement with Council Management Team IT Services has been fast tracking the implementation of Microsoft Teams with support of key suppliers e.g. Microsoft.

As part of a “Fast Track approach” it is possible that some users will experience disruption to service (by this we don’t mean key systems will fail), please see example below.

For example; some customers have access to be able to use the “Send from” function, when sending emails from another mail box. This functionality may not work immediately as we migrate customers.

Customers will still have access to read and view emails in these additional mailboxes and access will be restored during the migration.

Note: If we were to try and unpick the complexity of how our systems have been created in the past to ensure there was no disruption to service (as detailed above), it would take 3 months of analysis, and changes before being able to make headway for over 2,000 Customers to be able to have Microsoft Teams.

Workspace Laptop Software Upgrade Process

For Teams to work in an optimal way we need to apply a software upgrade to all Workspace laptops (this software has been specifically developed for our Council, and will be rolled out nationally at a later date).

Unfortunately, we are unable to currently upgrade Workspace laptops remotely (but after this upgrade all future Workspace software updates will be remotely deployed).

You will receive an e-mail from IT Services, with a link to an online booking system offering you several slots and dates (very similar to when you book your online shopping).

Please do not forward the invite e-mail on, as these will be sent in priority order as agreed with your service area leads.

Please do not contact IT Services, please contact your service area leads

You will be escorted to our upgrade centre, where you will be required to plug in your device and a technician will talk you through a few steps to complete the upgrade.

We expect the whole process to be completed in less than 1 hour.

Using Microsoft Teams

Many customers will have already experienced Microsoft Teams either personally or professionally.

You may have also used similar software such as webex, skype and zoom etc.

You will be sent a link to the Microsoft Teams toolkit, in Evolve, where you will find links to simple users guides and also some excellent short 2 min videos that show how to undertake simple tasks e.g. arrange a team’s meeting etc.

Please also speak to your colleagues, it’s a great way of sharing good practise and also keeping in touch with others during this time of isolation.

Please only call the IT Service Desk if you are experiencing technical issues with Microsoft Teams.

For all training and guidance queries please refer to the Microsoft Teams toolkit, if you are unable to find what you are looking for then please try Microsoft Teams help and learning.
