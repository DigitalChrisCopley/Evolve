Apprenticeships

Apprenticeships are a great way to gain industry-recognised qualifications while gaining real work experience and earning a wage. Bradford Council's apprenticeships are coordinated by their Learning and Development Academy Team. You can contact the team by sending an email to apprenticeships@bradford.gov.uk. 

Skills for Work Apprenticeships

Skills for Work provides apprenticeships in the following areas:

  • Business Administration
  • Cleaning Services
  • Customer Service
  • Health and Social Care
  • Management
  • Public Service Operations
  • Team Leading

Click on an apprenticeship area for more information.

Starting an apprenticeship

If you already work for Bradford Council and you want to start an apprenticeship you should read our Learning and Development Policy document (this link will only work on the Council's network).

If you are not already a Council employee you can search for apprenticeship vacancies on https://bradford.engageats.co.uk where you can also sign up for email alerts.
