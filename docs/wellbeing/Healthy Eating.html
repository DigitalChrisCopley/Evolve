Healthy Eating

 

Eating well and having a healthy lifestyle can help us to feel our best and can make a big difference to our long-term health.

Download the Eatwell Guide to see the proportions in which different types of foods are needed to have a well-balanced and healthy diet.

The Eatwell Guide applies to most people regardless of weight, dietary restrictions/preferences or ethnic origin.

You can also download the Adult Weekly Meal Plan which has seven days' worth of healthy eating.
